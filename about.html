About G&F Autohaus

Founded with a mission to revolutionize how premium vehicles are bought and sold in Switzerland, G&F Autohaus AG has established itself as a trusted partner for discerning drivers seeking clarity in the automotive marketplace.

Our team combines decades of automotive expertise with modern technology to deliver transparent market valuations, seamless financing coordination, and personalized service.

Based in Root, Switzerland, we serve clients across the country with a focus on premium makes including BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, Tesla, and more.
